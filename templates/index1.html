Our Services

How can we assist you?


Audio Books


Target Audience: Visually Impaired Users


Want to listen to a book that you wanted to read since you were small? Just upload the pdf and enjoy the pleasures of reading.

We plan on coming up with a recommender system to help you get started soon!

Summary


Target Audience: Visually Impaired Users


Want to decide which book to read? We have used a scraper to provide you with summaries of a few books. Listen to them and go read the book of your choice.

New books will be added soon!

Social Distancing Tracker


Target Audience: Visually Impaired Users


Want to lead a normal life in this "new normal"? We have come up with a social distancing tracker which gives a warning alert when distance is less than minimum required.

Object detection and alert to be added soon!

Subtitled Live Lectures


Target Audience: Hearing Impaired Users


Find it hard to communicate and read other's lips behind their masks? Just click on start recording button and get text conversions of whatever is being spoken to you.

Text2Speech


Target Audience: Mute Users


Want to communicate to others just like everyone else does? Type in your thoughts and we will say it for you. Male and Female Versions Available

Different Language Translations to be added soon!

Lockdown due to COVID-19 made all professions work in an unfamiliar environment. This is made as an attempt to help and support a community of the disabled to ease their tasks for remote operations
